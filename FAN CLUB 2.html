About Manchester City

Founded in 1880 as St. Mark’s, Manchester City became a powerhouse in English football. Known as The Citizens or The Sky Blues, City has won 9 Premier League titles, 6 FA Cups, 8 EFL Cups, and the UEFA Champions League in 2023.

Fun Fact: Manchester City set a record with 100 points in the 2017-18 Premier League season!

Club Legends & Current Stars

Sergio Agüero

All-time top scorer for Manchester City with 260 goals in all competitions. Known for his iconic “Aguerooo” goal that won City the 2011-12 Premier League.

Fun Fact: Agüero has five Premier League Golden Boot awards and scored the fastest hat-trick in Premier League history (180 seconds!).

Yaya Touré

Midfield maestro, scoring vital goals and controlling the tempo of games. Helped City win multiple Premier League titles and domestic cups.

Fun Fact: Yaya is one of the few players to score in five consecutive FA Cup Finals.

Erling Haaland

Current star striker known for his speed, power, and deadly finishing. Broke multiple Premier League scoring records in his debut season.

Fun Fact: Haaland scored 36 goals in 35 Premier League games in his first season, the fastest to reach 20 league goals in Premier League history.

Kevin De Bruyne

Premier League’s creative genius. Known for precise passing, long-range shots, and key assists. Multiple-time Playmaker of the Season.

Fun Fact: Kevin has assisted over 120 Premier League goals and is one of the best midfielders in the world.

Phil Foden

Young prodigy of Manchester City, versatile in attack and midfield. Already a Premier League winner and FA Cup champion.

Fun Fact: Phil scored the winning goal in the 2023 FA Cup final and is tipped as a future club legend.
